NIATEC Invitational Collegiate Cyber Defense Competition

An Alternative Approach to Cyber Defense Competitions

Red Team

Defend your network against the Red Team. Defend high value assets in order to gain points. Experience incident response in a safe yet intense environment.

Custom Scenario

Challenge your skills in a unique scenario that will be entertaining as well as educational. Immersive yourself in an environment to simulate the cybersecurity field.

Scoring Engine

Compete with your team while our proprietary Scoring Engine tracks and scores your progress in real time. Keep your critical systems online while your adversaries activley attempt to thwart your efforts.

Who are we?

We are NIATEC, the National Information Assurance Training and Education Center. Our mission is to provide outstanding academic opportunities that develop educated workers for government, academia, and industry. We plan, develop, and execute of the NIATEC Invitational Collegiate Cyber Defense Competition (NICCDC).
