Stay Connected With NIATEC

National Information Assurance Training and Education Center (NIATEC) is a consortium of academic, industry, and government organizations to help improve the literacy, awareness, training, and education standards in Information Assurance. The cyber space is constantly changing and shifting. NIATEC is in the forefront of developing the next generation of cybersecuirty professionals. Stay up to date with the latest NIATEC and NICCDC news by subscribing to our newsletter.

Who are we?

We are NIATEC, the National Information Assurance Training and Education Center. Our mission is to provide outstanding academic opportunities that develop educated workers for government, academia, and industry. We plan, develop, and execute of the NIATEC Invitational Collegiate Cyber Defense Competition (NICCDC).
