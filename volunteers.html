Volunteer Opportunities

Support the Best Cyber Defense Competition in the Mountain West

We constantly need help to keep all aspects of the NICCDC running. We are always looking for volunteers to help out in many different ways. Volunteering for the NICCDC is a great way to get experience and boost your resume. This is an excellent opportunity to meet and help train our next generation of cyber defenders. Volunteers can serve for a single day, multiple days, or for the entire competition. Special opportunities are available to students who are looking for semester long internships. To ease scheduling and the familiarization process we ask volunteers to cover a 3 hour block of time when possible during a NICCDC event.

Unlike traditional "hack and defend" or "capture the flag" contests, this competition tests each team's ability to operate, secure, manage, and maintain a corporate network while making mission oriented decisions. This competition seeks to emulate a realistic, while enjoyable, corporate administration and security experience - giving the competitors a chance to compare their education and training against their peers and the real world challenges that await them.

We Need Your Help

A small cadre of dedicated volunteers helps keep the NICCDC running smoothly. We always need volunteers. We need volunteers to help with administrative tasks, our infrastructure, volunteer judges to evaluate participants, and even volunteers to function as members of our red team. If you would like to volunteer please click the link below and fill out our survey. For any questions or concerns regarding this compeition, please contact the Cyber Range Director by email niccdc@niatec.isu.edu or by phone at (208) 282-3563.

Who are we?

We are NIATEC, the National Information Assurance Training and Education Center. Our mission is to provide outstanding academic opportunities that develop educated workers for government, academia, and industry. We plan, develop, and execute of the NIATEC Invitational Collegiate Cyber Defense Competition (NICCDC).
