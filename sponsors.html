Sponsors

NICCDC sponsors keep the lights on and the network traffic flowing. Our sponsors and supporters play a large role in our compeittion events. Students who participate in the NICCDC are the top in the region, having been invited or having applied to participate meeting qualification standards. The NICCDC typically hosts a recruiting mixer designed to link participants with potential employers and internships. Our sponsors are invited and encouraged to participate in this unique opportunity to meet and recruit particpants.

Who are we?

We are NIATEC, the National Information Assurance Training and Education Center. Our mission is to provide outstanding academic opportunities that develop educated workers for government, academia, and industry. We plan, develop, and execute of the NIATEC Invitational Collegiate Cyber Defense Competition (NICCDC).
