Request a NIATEC Professional to Speak at Your Organization

In Person or Online

Today's world is incredibly complex. The National Information Assurance Training and Education Center has the daunting task of preparing the next generation of cybersecurity professionals to enter the fray. The NIATEC speaker program introduces you to our people and our mission. NIATEC professionals are trained and experienced in cybersecurity and information assurance topics. We have been invited to guest lecture at the University of Iceland, Brigham Young University, the College of Idaho, and others. We can provide training and tabletop exercises to help your students and employees to expose them to cybersecurity topics.

Our team members are immensely proud of their role in the mission of NIATEC and their efforts to strengthen the national defense structure. Our goal is to identify opportunities for NIATEC professionals to help audiences understand the intricacies of today's information and cyber environments. Our speakers let you hear directly from our team about topics that are relevant to everyone in today's fast changing cyber-landscape.

Who are we?

We are NIATEC, the National Information Assurance Training and Education Center. Our mission is to provide outstanding academic opportunities that develop educated workers for government, academia, and industry. We plan, develop, and execute of the NIATEC Invitational Collegiate Cyber Defense Competition (NICCDC).
